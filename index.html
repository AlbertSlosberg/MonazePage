what  is our brand about   

We all are used to think that watches are not only about style and fashion but also money.  Monaze wants to prove that watch you wear (or will) is not just indicator of status and index of richness it is something that reflects your personality or even your current mood. We don't compare everything to money!      

 Monaze is a mix of vintage and modern. However vintage is not about old her, it is about timeless styles and quality. Where modern means that we can be relevant in the past, in the presen, and in the future.
